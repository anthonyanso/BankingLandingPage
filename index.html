Our Story

Founded in 1995, Trevorobank has been serving communities with integrity and innovation for nearly three decades. What started as a small community bank has grown into a trusted financial institution serving thousands of customers nationwide.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide accessible, reliable, and personalized banking services to individuals and businesses. Today, we continue to uphold these values while embracing cutting-edge technology to enhance your banking experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common banking questions

How do I open a new account?

You can open a new account online, by visiting any branch, or by calling our customer service team. You'll need a valid ID, proof of address, and an initial deposit to get started.

Is mobile banking secure?

Yes, our mobile banking uses bank-level encryption and multi-factor authentication to protect your information. We also monitor accounts 24/7 for suspicious activity.

What are your loan interest rates?

Interest rates vary based on the type of loan, your credit score, and current market conditions. Contact us for personalized rate quotes for auto loans, mortgages, or personal loans.

How can I avoid ATM fees?

Use any of our 50,000+ partner ATMs nationwide for free withdrawals. You can also get cash back at participating retailers or visit any of our branch locations.
