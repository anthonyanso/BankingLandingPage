Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What documents do I need to open an account?

You'll need a valid government-issued ID, proof of address (utility bill or lease), and your Social Security number. For business accounts, additional documentation may be required.

How can I access my account online?

You can access your account through our website or mobile app using your username and password. If you haven't registered yet, visit any branch or call customer service.

What are your loan interest rates?

Interest rates vary based on loan type, credit score, and market conditions. Contact us for current rates and to discuss your specific situation with a loan specialist.

Is my money safe with Trevorobank?

Yes, all deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor. We also use advanced security measures to protect your personal and financial information.
